What the app does
  • Asks: "What are you bringing to God?"
  • Matches scripture, prayers, hymns, and songs to your emotional state
  • Tracks prayer journeys: answered, ongoing, or changed
  • Offers AI-assisted prayer writing and reflection summaries

Core Experience

A gentle flow from emotion to prayer to perspective

LetGod is built for believers who want faithful guidance without noise, pressure, or complicated setup.

Emotional check-ins

Start each session by naming what you are carrying, then receive spiritually relevant guidance for that moment.

Personalized spiritual resources

Discover scripture, songs, hymns, Bible stories, and prayers aligned to your current emotional and spiritual state.

Prayer journal

Capture prayers over time and mark follow-ups as answered, ongoing, or changed to see God’s faithfulness clearly.

Reflection insights

Review descriptive summaries that highlight recurring themes, gratitude shifts, and long-term spiritual patterns.

Anonymous prayer garden

Optionally share anonymous prayer requests in a moderated space for encouragement and community intercession.

Premium features

Unlock deeper AI-assisted reflections, richer summaries, advanced habits, and additional personalized prayer tools.
