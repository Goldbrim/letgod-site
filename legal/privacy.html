Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LetGod collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information when you use our mobile app, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account details such as name, email address, and password (stored securely, not in plain text).
  • Profile information you choose to add.
  • Journal entries, reflections, prayer notes, and other in-app content you submit.
  • Support and compliance messages you send to us.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and app usage data such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, and timestamps.
  • Log data such as IP address, crash diagnostics, and basic performance metrics.
  • Approximate location derived from IP address for security and fraud prevention.

1.3 Information from third parties

If you sign in through a third-party provider (if available), we may receive basic profile identifiers permitted by that provider.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Authenticate your account and secure our systems.
  • Deliver app features such as reminders, saved entries, and personalized experiences.
  • Respond to support requests and compliance inquiries.
  • Investigate abuse, enforce our Terms, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Generate de-identified or aggregated insights to improve product quality.

3. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where required by law, we process personal data under one or more legal bases: contractual necessity, legitimate interests, consent, and legal compliance.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only as needed with:

  • Service providers that support hosting, analytics, authentication, customer support, and security.
  • Authorities or legal counterparts when required by law or valid legal process.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, subject to lawful safeguards.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes in this Policy, including compliance, security, dispute resolution, and recordkeeping obligations. Deleted account data is removed or anonymized according to our deletion workflow and backup lifecycle.

6. Your rights and choices

  • Access and update profile information in your account settings.
  • Manage reminder and marketing preferences.
  • Request account deletion through in-app settings or our public process.
  • Request a copy of personal data where legally required.
  • Object to or request restriction of certain processing where applicable.

See our Account & Data Deletion page for detailed steps.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law). If we learn that personal information from a child was collected without proper authorization, we will delete it.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use safeguards intended to provide lawful transfer protections.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be posted with a revised effective date and, when required, additional notice.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact hello@letgod.app. For general compliance matters, visit Contact & Compliance.

This policy is provided as general information and does not constitute legal advice.
