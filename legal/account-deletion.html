Account & Data Deletion

Effective date: April 19, 2026

LetGod provides account deletion controls to help users permanently close accounts and remove associated personal data.

1. Delete your account inside the app

  1. Open LetGod and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile -> Settings -> Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Review the deletion summary and confirm with your password.

2. Request deletion without app access

If you cannot access the app, send a request to hello@letgod.app from your account email with subject line: Account Deletion Request.

To protect users, we may ask for additional verification before deleting an account.

3. What is deleted

  • Profile data tied to your account.
  • Journal entries, reflections, and prayer logs linked to your profile.
  • User-specific app settings and preferences.

4. What may be retained temporarily

  • Security and fraud-prevention records.
  • Transaction or billing records where legally required.
  • Limited server backups for a short rolling period before automatic purge.

Retained records are kept only as required by law, security needs, or legitimate compliance obligations.

5. Deletion timeline

  • In-app deletion request acknowledgment: immediate confirmation screen.
  • Primary account deactivation: typically within 24 hours.
  • Full deletion from active systems: typically within 30 days.
  • Backup lifecycle purge: up to 90 days, subject to secure backup rotation.

6. Data download requests

Where required by law, users may request a copy of personal data before deletion by contacting hello@letgod.app.

7. Questions

For deletion status or identity verification support, contact hello@letgod.app or visit Contact & Compliance.
